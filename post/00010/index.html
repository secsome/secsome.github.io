· 2020-03-24 ·

构造是一种通过观察式子结构从而起到简化运算的重要方法...

例一

设a>b>0,n∈N∗,An=1n+1(an+an−1b+an−2b2+⋯+abn−1+bn),Bn=(a+b2)n.a>b>0,n\in\mathbb{N^*},A_n=\dfrac{1}{n+1}\left(a^n+a^{n-1}b+a^{n-2}b^2+\cdots+ab^{n-1}+b^n\right),B_n=\left(\dfrac{a+b}2\right)^n.a>b>0,n∈N∗,An​=n+11​(an+an−1b+an−2b2+⋯+abn−1+bn),Bn​=(2a+b​)n.求证：An>BnA_n>B_nAn​>Bn​

解法一（常规解法）

令a+b2=x,a−b2=y,\dfrac{a+b}2=x,\dfrac{a-b}2=y,2a+b​=x,2a−b​=y,则

{An=(x+y)n+1−(x−y)n+12(n+1)yBn=xn\begin{cases} A_n=\dfrac{(x+y)^{n+1}-(x-y)^{n+1}}{2(n+1)y}\\ B_n=x^n\end{cases} ⎩⎨⎧​An​=2(n+1)y(x+y)n+1−(x−y)n+1​Bn​=xn​

因此

An=Cn+11xny+Cn+13xn−2y3+⋯(n+1)y>Cn+11xny(n+1)y=xn=Bn.A_n=\dfrac{\mathop{\rm C}\nolimits_ {n+1}^1x^ny+\mathop{\rm C}\nolimits_{n+1}^3x^{n-2}y^3+\cdots}{(n+1)y}> \dfrac{\mathop{\rm C}\nolimits_{n+1}^1x^ny}{(n+1)y}=x^n=B_n. An​=(n+1)yCn+11​xny+Cn+13​xn−2y3+⋯​>(n+1)yCn+11​xny​=xn=Bn​.

解法二（构造函数）

An>Bn  ⟸  (a+b)n>(n+1)(a+b2)n  ⟸  an+1−bn+1a−b>(n+1)⋅(a+b2)n\begin{aligned} &A_n>B_n\\ \impliedby &(a+b)^n>(n+1)\left(\dfrac{a+b}2\right)^n\\ \impliedby &\dfrac{a^{n+1}-b^{n+1}}{a-b}>(n+1)\cdot \left(\dfrac{a+b}2\right)^n\\ \end{aligned} ⟸⟸​An​>Bn​(a+b)n>(n+1)(2a+b​)na−ban+1−bn+1​>(n+1)⋅(2a+b​)n​
